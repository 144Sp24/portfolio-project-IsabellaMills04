Hello! I'm Isabella Mills. I'm currently a freshman at Northwest Missouri State University studying Digital Media with an emphasis in Computer Science!

People often describe me as a very creative person. For as long as I can remember, I've loved art, designing things, and anything that gets the creative gears going!

All throughout my high school career, I took various art classes to enhance my skills so that my senior year, I'd be able to take an Advanced Placement art course. Additonally, my senior year I decided to join my school's yearbook as a photographer. My mother has had a digital camera for years that I've always loved tinkering with, so with the knowledge of art principles and camera mechanics, I was able to produce quality photos for the yearbook.
